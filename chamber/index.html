Services

Welcome to Campos do Jordão Chamber of Commerce! We are dedicated to providing a wide range of services to support businesses and promote economic growth in our vibrant city nestled in the heart of nature.

Business Networking Opportunities

Join our thriving business community and unlock endless networking opportunities. Connect with local entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and potential partners to expand your professional network and foster collaborations.

Business Development Resources

Access valuable resources and tools to help your business thrive. From market research and business planning to funding opportunities and regulatory guidance, our expert team is here to support your growth and success.

Advocacy and Representation

We advocate for the interests of local businesses and represent our members' voices in matters affecting the business community. From policy advocacy to community engagement, we work tirelessly to create a favorable business environment for all.

Training and Workshops

Stay ahead of the curve with our training programs and workshops designed to enhance your skills and knowledge. From leadership development to digital marketing strategies, our educational offerings cater to businesses of all sizes and industries.

Promotion and Marketing Assistance

Gain visibility and reach new customers with our promotion and marketing assistance services. Whether you're launching a new product or hosting an event, we provide support to help you effectively market your business and attract customers.

Business Support Services

From legal and financial assistance to administrative support, our chamber offers a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of businesses. Let us be your trusted partner on your journey to success.

Explore these services and more with Campos do Jordão Chamber of Commerce. Join us today and unlock the full potential of your business!

Events

Campos do Jordao Winter Festival

Join us for the enchanting Campos do Jordao Winter Festival, a celebration of art and music amidst the stunning winter landscape. Experience world-class performances, indulge in local cuisine, and immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural atmosphere. This event is a perfect blend of entertainment and the natural beauty that Campos do Jordao has to offer.

Spring Blossom Celebration

Celebrate the arrival of spring in Campos do Jordao with our Spring Blossom Celebration. Enjoy a weekend filled with colorful blooms, outdoor activities, and a festive atmosphere. Participate in flower exhibitions, cultural performances, and explore the breathtaking scenery as the city comes alive with the beauty of blooming flowers.

Campos do Jordao Chocolate Festival

Indulge your sweet tooth at the Campos do Jordao Chocolate Festival. Immerse yourself in a chocolate lover's paradise, featuring artisanal chocolatiers, chocolate-themed workshops, and delectable treats from local vendors. This event is a delightful celebration of the city's rich chocolate heritage, offering a sweet experience for visitors of all ages.
